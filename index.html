    Lighting

    Three ways to color the board. Pick by how many colors you want.

    Which one?
    • Built-in — one color, runs on the board, keeps going after you close this.
    • Software — two colors, quick strobe / cycle / breathe, from the browser.
    • Palettes — two to six colors, save your own presets, from the browser.

    Built-in effects

    The keyboard's own RGB effects. They run on the board and keep going after you close this. Each takes one color (or its own rainbow).

    Customize effects
    Pick which effects show in the dropdown above. Firmware still supports all of them — this only tidies the list.
    The single color for the effect above. Reactive effects (Splash, Solid Reactive) ripple in this color; rainbow/cycle effects ignore it.

    Side LED bar

    The three LEDs in the side light bar. Give them their own color, independent of the key effect above — or untick to let them follow the keys. Saved to the keyboard.

    Software effects — two or more colors

    These do what the built-in effects can't: multiple colors. They run from the browser (not the keyboard), whole-board (not per-key reactive), and stream live — so they stop on Stop, on leaving this tab, or on disconnect. One plays at a time.
